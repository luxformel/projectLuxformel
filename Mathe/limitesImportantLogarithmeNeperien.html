Limites Important

  • $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} \ln(x) = \lim_{x \rightarrow 0^+} \ln(x)$$ = + \infty $$

    et

    $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow + \infty} \ln(x) = + \infty $$
  • $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow 1} \frac{\ln(x)}{x-1} = 1$$

    et

    $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\ln(x + 1)}{x} = 1$$
  • $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow + \infty} \frac{\ln(x)}{x} = 0 $$

    et

    $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} x \ln(x) = 0$$
  • $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow + \infty} \frac{x}{\ln(x)} = + \infty $$

  • $\forall n \in \mathbb{N^*},$

    $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow + \infty} \frac{\ln(x)}{x^n} = 0 $$ $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} x^n \ln (x) = 0 $$ $$ \lim_{x \rightarrow + \infty} \frac{x^n}{\ln(x)} = 0 $$
